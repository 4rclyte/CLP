Table Top Saturday

Clear off a sitting space and grab yourself a drink. It may be a while before we can begin.

This game night is usually hosted at Christian's place in Fincastle. Other hosts have been Andy and Em at their house somewhere on 1st street. There's always a handful of options every night depending on what is brought and how many people we need to accomodate.

Some favorites include Sherriff of Nottingham, Magic the Gathering: Commander, Dungeons & Dragons, Settlers of Cattan, Boss Monster, Super Smash Bros. Wii U / 3DS & Exploding Kittens.
